## How to install:
 1. `curl -sS https://get.pimoroni.com/touchphat > script.sh`
 2. `nano script.sh`
 3. Replace 8.8.8.8 with www.ic.ac.uk
 4. `chmod 775 script.sh`
 5. `./script.sh`
